Rotaract Club of Thane North

We’re thrilled that you stopped by! We are a community service club for college students and young professionals (16–35) based in Thane, Maharashtra. We focus on community service, professional development and fellowship.

Rotaract is an international program founded in 1968 by Rotary International that fosters leadership, responsible citizenship and a spirit of service. We are proud to be part of Rotary International (RID 3142).

About Us

The Rotaract Club of Thane North is part of Rotary International (RID 3142). We lead and support community projects focused on health, education, the environment, and youth development.

Our Mission

To empower young adults to create sustainable community impact through service, professional growth, and meaningful fellowship.
